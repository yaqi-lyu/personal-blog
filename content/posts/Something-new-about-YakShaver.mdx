The idea of YakShaver sounds awesome to me. Writing detailed bug reports usually takes a lot of time and effort. Having tools like YakShaver to do this work for me makes things much easier and faster.

## Setting Up YakShaver

First, I completed the basic settings to get everything ready. I linked my GitHub account to YakShaver, which was quite straightforward. Then I added the YakShaver bot to my team's workspace. The setup process didn't take very long, and the instructions were clear enough to follow without any major problems.

## How I Use It

When I want YakShaver to process my recordings, the process is simple. I just talk to the bot and tell it to switch to "on" mode. Then I start recording my screen while I explain the issue or demonstrate the bug. That's basically it. The recording doesn't need to be perfect or formal. I can just speak naturally and show what's happening on my screen.

After I finish recording, the next thing for me is simply to wait. YakShaver processes the video in the background. When it's done, I get a notification message from the bot. Then I just need to go to the related project and double-check the Product Backlog Item (PBI) that YakShaver has created.

## A Useful Feature

One feature I really appreciate is the ability to set nicknames for team members. This means when I'm recording the video, I can use people's usual nicknames instead of their full formal names. It makes the whole process feel more natural, like I'm just having a normal conversation with my team. YakShaver understands these nicknames and correctly identifies who I'm talking about in the final report.

## Why It Works for Me

The main reason YakShaver helps me so much is that it removes the language barrier I usually face. When I record a video, I can focus on showing the problem clearly rather than worrying about grammar or finding the right technical words in English. YakShaver watches my recording, understands what I'm explaining, and then writes everything in proper, professional language.This saves me a significant amount of time.
